The facts

Human society is very unequal and destroy its environment.
Yet individually, we want a society that is fair, fraternal, and respectful of the environment.
I do not agree with those facts

Reasons of the paradox

Collective action produces effects opposed to individual interests for two reasons:
  • In collectivity, individual interest express itself through representation
  • Collective actions are hidden
I do not agree

Some solutions

Let organize transparent collective actions whose production will benefit all. Examples :
  • Create transparent companies (contractual documents , accounting, bank accounts,...) whose intellectual production are free (no patent)
  • Create transparent banks, offering universal and free (e-)payment card, with no transactionf fee
I do not agree
