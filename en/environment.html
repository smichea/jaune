Environment

It is constituted of things that are exterior but surround us, wether they are natural or artificial (produced by humans). Environment is also limited to the space in which we evolve and that we use for our needs. Mars planet for instance is not (yet) part of our environment, even though it contains man made objects.

Environment destruction

Humans,as clever as he could be, is not safe to harm his environment in a fatal way.
  • Mass destruction weapons (atomic, chemical, biological,..)
  • Depletion of natural resources
  • Pollution
  • Deforestation
  • Biodiversity loss
  • Wastes accumulation
