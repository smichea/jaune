I) Bank project

Currentky major banks do not allow to the organization that would like to be transparent, to give a visualization access to their bank account.
Organization could release their bank statements, but nothing can prove the document would be originals.
In the majority of cases, we use a bank to open an account or to pay what we need.
It is therefore only those base operations that need to be implemented in a first phase.
More precisely :
  • Be able to receive his salary or get paid for invoices by wire transfer.
  • Be able to wire transfer to other bank accounts.
  • Be able to pay by credit card or electronic way.
  • Allow users that are willing to, to give a public visualization access to their account.
  • Implement the governance of the bank as a liquid democarcy.
  • Provide all base services as an API so that third party organization can use them to build added value services
