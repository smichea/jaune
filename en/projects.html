Yellow label

The yellow committee awards a label to any organization (association, company, institution, administration,...) that respects the following criteria (coming from the manifest) :
  • Transparency criteria: All financial transactions are publicly released, in real time, all documents, messages are produced under copyleft licence and publicly released.
  • Democracy criteria: Decisions are taken in a democratic way, anyone being able to delegate and take back his vote as he wish (liquid democracy).
  • Optimization criteria: All precautions are taken to limit the negative impact on the environment.
  • Propagation criteria: Whenever possible, every partner or provider is also bearer of the yellow label.
All the projects listed in this page are either awardees of the yellow label or are in conception stage but commit to do their best to obtain the yellow label.
